      1.   A clock is started at noon. By 10 minutes past 5, the hour hand has turned through:
      A.145º
      B.150º
      C.155º
      D.160º
      Answer: Option C
      Explanation:
      Angle traced by hour hand in 12 hrs = 360º.
      Angle traced by hour hand in 5 hrs 10 min.
      31/6hrs = ((360/12)x(31/6))º= 155º.
