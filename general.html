      General Science

    • ->General knowledge has been defined in differential psychology as "culturally valued knowledge communicated by a range of non-specialist media" and encompassing a wide subject range.

    -> This definition excludes highly specialized learning that can only be obtained with extensive training and information confined to a single medium.

    -> General knowledge is an important component of crystallized intelligence and is strongly associated with general intelligence, and with openness to experience.
